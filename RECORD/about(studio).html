About De Flame

            Tinyiko manzini also known as DeFlame aka TKVY aka Real baby(Booby) is a South African rapper and producer,
            born in Gauteng Pretoria.DeFlame was born in 2006 February 23rd.
            
            He grew up listening to Hip Hop ata very young age and it  inspired him to start making his own music.He
            started making music with his best G Big Boy Poseidon in the fifth grade,they barely knew anything about production
            but still wrote lyrics in class with no beat and no instrumental what so ever.Flame started producing ata the age of 13 and 
            started recording at the the age of 14.
            
            He made beats for rappers such as Kals,Big Boy Poseidon,Risso and Deete. He had his first feature from Big Boy Poseidon(No space Remix and Blue Brick)
            DeFlame is hoping that things will now move forward in terms of his career and be successful and take care of his 
            family for generations to come.
